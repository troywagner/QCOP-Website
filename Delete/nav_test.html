Delegates

If you want to make a difference in the world, you've come to the right place. Queen's Conference on Philanthropy is unique in Canada – the first undergraduate conference dedicated to philanthropy. Over 100 student delegates from all over Canada attend the two–day conference, eager to explore what being a philanthropist means and how they can impact their communities. No matter what you're excited about, from empowering fellow students to networking with philanthropy–minded business executives, QCOP's dynamic speakers and workshops will leave you inspired and prepared to make an impact.

So what makes the perfect QCOP delegate? We're looking for people who are...

  • PASSIONATE about making a difference
  • INTERESTING and CREATIVE
  • READY to impact the world around them

Sound like the perfect opportunity for you? Here's what you need to know:
QCOP 2015 will be held at the Holiday Inn Waterfront Hotel in Kingston, ON, from January 23-25, 2015. Delegate applications will be launched soon, so keep checking our website and follow us on Facebook to stay updated!

We can't wait to share this year's conference with you, and don't hesitate to contact us if you have any questions!
