Queen's Conference on Philanthropy is Canada's premier undergraduate philanthropic conference. It aims to educate, inspire and empower top undergraduate delegates nationwide to make a difference in today's world.

QCOP 2015 will feature a lineup of accomplished and motivational speakers who work across different industries, and welcome over 150 delegates from across Canada. Delegates will participate in a series of hands-on workshops to provide them with tools to address, analyze, and tackle both local and global issues.

The QCOP Vision

We believe in a world where people help each other. We believe in "Paying it Forward", in #nicenominations, and in smiling at strangers on the street. As the first undergraduate conference of our kind we continue to grow and inspire students from across Canada. QCOP 2015 features a new theme, and will premiere a series of new events that will take this year's conference to a new level.
